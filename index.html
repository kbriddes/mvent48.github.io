SPACE RACE:
When running and gunning is your only option


Introduction to the Universe

Space Race pits players all across the world in a race to the ultimate prize: the truth. Why are we here? Where are we going? When will it end? Space Race is an action-packed, RPG with the objective of racing to the center of the universe to find these truths.
